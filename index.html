FAQ

Dealer Partner FAQ.

Straight answers - no fine print, no corporate speak. If it doesn’t work in your real world, it doesn’t work for us either.

Are we liable if something goes wrong with the transport?
No. We operate as the transport broker and take responsibility for logistics, communication, and carrier coordination. You’re simply connecting your customer with us - we stand behind the shipment.
Do we have to sign a long-term contract?
No contracts or minimums required. You can test us on one customer, or make us your go-to for every out-of-state buyer. We earn our place with performance, not paperwork.
Can we still use our existing transport contacts?
Absolutely. Many dealers use us as their primary solution and keep others as backup — others do the opposite. You decide where we fit.
How and when do we get the $100 referral bonus?
After the vehicle is delivered and confirmed, we send your $100 referral bonus within 24–48 hours via Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, or check - whichever you prefer.
What routes do you cover?
We ship nationwide - from any state to any state. Open and enclosed carriers are available depending on the vehicle, value, and your customer’s preference.
Who talks to the customer after we refer them?
We do. Once you connect us, we handle all transport-related communication so your team isn’t tied up answering questions about drivers, timing, or delivery.
